Top Destinations


Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Karnataka.


Goa

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India within the Konkan region, geographically separated from the Deccan highlands by the Western Ghats.[8][9] It is located between the Indian states of Maharashtra to the north and Karnataka to the east and south, with the Arabian Sea forming its western coast.


Mumbai

Mumbai, the official name until 1995) is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra and the de facto financial centre of India.


Andaman

The Andaman Islands are an archipelago in the northeastern Indian Ocean about 130 km (81 mi) southwest off the coasts of Myanmar's Ayeyarwady Region. Together with the Nicobar Islands to their south, the Andamans serve as a maritime boundary between the Bay of Bengal to the west and the Andaman Sea to the east.

FAQs

How do I make a flight booking on FlyHigh.com?

A: You can book a flight on FlyHigh.com in five easy steps: Head over to the FlyHigh.com flight booking page, Enter your departure and arrival destinations, Select your air travel dates, Choose from our wide range of flights based on your airfare preferences, Click on ‘Book Now’ and your air flight booking is done. Alternatively, you can also use the MakeMyTrip app for your flight ticket booking. Download the MakeMyTrip app, Put in the details i.e. date of journey, departure and arrival destinations, travel class of your choice, Select on your best comfortable option and click on 'Book Now'.

Why could I not avail the flight booking offers at the time of checkout?

Can I avail domestic flight offers on FlyHigh.com?

Of course, you can. While making domestic flight bookings, you can avail any special offer that is active at that time. In accordance with the offer selected, a listing of eligible flights would show up on your screen. You can then apply the price filter and click on the downwards arrow, following which budget-friendly flights would start showing up in ascending order from the top (lowest price on top).

How can I avail budget air tickets on FlyHigh.com?

It’s super-easy to avail budget airfare while booking your flight tickets on FlyHigh.com. Just select the ‘Price’ filter once the available flight options are displayed and adjust according to your convenience.
